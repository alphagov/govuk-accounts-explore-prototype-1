Proving your identity with GOV.UK Verify

GOV.UK Verify is a secure way to prove who you are online. It helps protect you against online identity theft.

The Disclosure and Barring Service uses Verify to stop other people getting a criminal record check using your details, as it stops someone from pretending to be you.

You'll need:

  • a valid passport or
  • a valid driving licence, full or provisional, with your photo on it
