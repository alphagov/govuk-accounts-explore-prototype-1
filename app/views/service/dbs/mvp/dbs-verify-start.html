Prove your identity with GOV.UK Verify

GOV.UK Verify is a safe and secure way to prove your identity online.

It will take you around 15 minutes to prove your identity.

Using GOV.UK Verify is different to proving your identity with a GOV.UK account.

You'll need either:

  • a valid passport
  • a full or provisional valid driving licence

We only use the personal information you give us to prove that you are who you say you are.
