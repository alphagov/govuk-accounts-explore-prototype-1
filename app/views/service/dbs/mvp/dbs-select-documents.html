Select your identity documents

Select which of the following documents you have to hand and would be able to take to the Post Office within the next 10 days.

You must select at least 3 documents. These can be a combination of documents from any of the 3 document lists.

The documents must:

  • be in your current name (except your birth certificate, which is also acceptable in a different name)
  • be in your current address (where an address is included)
  • include your date of birth (at least 1 document should include this)
  • be original documents (you cannot use photocopies)
