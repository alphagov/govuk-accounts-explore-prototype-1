Validating your identity at the Post Office

To validate your identity at the Post Office you need to follow 3 steps:

  1. Select the identity documents you have to hand from our list.

  2. Print the letter we'll generate for you after you complete the rest of your application.

  3. Take your documents and the letter to a post office that offers the DBS ID validation service within 10 days (we'll generate a list of your nearest post offices at the end of this application).
