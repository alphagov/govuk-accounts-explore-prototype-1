The way you subscribe to get emails from GOV.UK is changing

You need a GOV.UK account to get these emails.

GOV.​UK accounts are new

In the future, you'll be able to use your GOV.UK account to sign in to most services on GOV.UK.

Currently GOV.UK accounts are separate from other government accounts (for example Government Gateway or Universal Credit).

At the moment you can only use your GOV.UK account with a few services.

If you have other GOV.UK email subscriptions

If you have subscriptions to other GOV.UK topics or pages, these will be moved to your new account so you can manage all your subscriptions in one place.

Create a GOV.UK account or sign in

You need a UK mobile number to create an account.
