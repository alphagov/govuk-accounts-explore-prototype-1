The way you subscribe to emails from GOV.UK is changing

You need a GOV.UK account to get these emails.

GOV.​UK accounts are new

They are a first step towards creating a single account that you can use to sign in to all government services.

GOV.UK accounts will eventually replace all other government accounts.

At the moment, GOV.UK accounts are separate from other government accounts (for example Government Gateway or Universal Credit).

Currently, you can only use a GOV.UK account to manage your GOV.UK email subscriptions.

We’ll be adding more features and services.

If you have other GOV.UK email subscriptions

If you have subscriptions to other GOV.UK topics or pages, these will be moved to your new account so you can manage all your subscriptions in one place.
