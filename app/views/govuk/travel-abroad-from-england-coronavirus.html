Paying the charge

You must pay the Dartford Crossing charge by midnight the day after you cross. You can get a fine if you’re late or do not pay.

You can also use this service to:

  • make a one-off payment
  • set up a pay as you go Dart Charge account
  • set up a local resident discount if you live in Dartford or Thurrock
  • pay or challenge a fine (known as a Penalty Charge Notice)
You need a GOV.UK account to set up a Dart Charge account. If you do not have a GOV.UK account, you'll be asked to create one. You'll need a UK mobile number.

Contact Dart Charge online if you need any help using the service.

Other ways to pay

You can:

  • pay with cash at your nearest Payzone store
  • set up an account by post
  • pay in advance or top-up your account by post
  • pay or set up an account by phone
