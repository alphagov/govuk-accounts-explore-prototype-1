Some pages of GOV.UK have links to ‘get emails’ or ‘get emails about this topic’.

You can get emails about updates to:

  • topics like money, childcare or education
  • pages about departments, agencies and public bodies
  • foreign travel advice pages
  • news and communications

You can also get emails about:

  • marine accident reports
  • railway accident reports

How to change your email preferences

You can unsubscribe or change your email preferences at any time.

You can also use the ‘unsubscribe’ or ‘change your email preferences’ links in the emails you get from GOV.UK to:

  • unsubscribe from some or all emails
  • change how often you get emails
  • change your email address

Problems with GOV.UK emails

If you’re having problems getting emails from GOV.UK, check your spam or junk folder.

If this does not work, contact GOV.UK support for help.
