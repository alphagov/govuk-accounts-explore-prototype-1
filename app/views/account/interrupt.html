The way you subscribe to emails from GOV.UK is changing

You need a new GOV.UK account to get these emails.

GOV.​UK accounts are new

GOV.UK accounts are a first step towards creating a single account where you can access all your government services.

GOV.UK accounts are different from other government accounts you may have used before (for example Government Gateway or Universal Credit).

At the moment, you can only use a GOV.UK account to:

  • manage the emails you get from GOV.UK
  • save answers to the Brexit checker

We’ll be adding more features and services in the next few months.

Create a new GOV.UK account

It takes about 5 minutes to create an account.

You’ll need:

  • an email address
  • a UK mobile number with reception
